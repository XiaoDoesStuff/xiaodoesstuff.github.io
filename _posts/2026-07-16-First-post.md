## Welcome to the Site

This is my first post on the new Jekyll + Minimal Mistakes setup. I’m using this space to document projects, experiments, and whatever tech adventures I get myself into.

I’ll be posting about:

- Homelab builds and upgrades  
- Retro tech repairs and restorations  
- Random automation projects  
- Thrift store rescues and odd finds  
- Anything else I feel like tinkering with  

## Why I’m Doing This

I wanted a simple, fast, and clean way to publish without dealing with heavy CMS platforms. Jekyll keeps things lightweight, and Minimal Mistakes gives me a layout that looks good without extra work.

## What’s Next

More posts soon — probably something involving Proxmox, retro hardware, or a weird device I found at a surplus sale. Stay tuned.
